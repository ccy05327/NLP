Topics 1-5: Foundations of NLP

An introduction to the fundamental concepts of natural language processing.

Learning Objectives

By the end of these foundational modules, you will understand the core concepts that underpin all modern NLP systems.

  • Understand the history and scope of natural language processing
  • Learn about text preprocessing and tokenization techniques
  • Explore language modeling and n-gram approaches
  • Apply basic statistical methods to text analysis
  • Understand evaluation methods for NLP systems

Topic Overview

Topic 1: Introduction to NLP

Overview of natural language processing, its applications, and the challenges of working with human language.

Topic 2: Text Preprocessing

Tokenization, normalization, stemming, lemmatization, and other essential text preparation techniques.

Topic 3: Language Models

N-gram models, probability estimation, smoothing techniques, and the foundations of statistical language modeling.

Topic 4: Text Classification

Naive Bayes, feature extraction, document classification, and sentiment analysis fundamentals.

Topic 5: Evaluation & Metrics

Precision, recall, F1-score, cross-validation, and other methods for evaluating NLP system performance.

Practical Applications

Hands-on exercises and real-world examples demonstrating the application of foundational NLP concepts.
